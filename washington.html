Why choose our Washington D.C. school?

Our English school in Washington puts you at the center of D.C. life, with iconic monuments and museums within walking distance. Washington D.C is a relatively small but diverse and dynamic city allowing for a unique environment to learn English in.

  • Choose the right flexible English course for you to allow you the right balance of studying and site seeing
  • Focus on you future with a Business English course  to prepare you for an international working environment
  • Improve your chances of getting a better score on the TOEFL, GMAT or GRE exams with an exam preparation course
  • Accommodation options include dedicated student residences just a short walk away from the school, or living with nearby local families
  • If you want to expand your site seeing beyond Washington, transport links can take you for weekend trips to nearby cities including New York City and Philadelphia
  • If you want to continue your studies at a US university, Washington D.C students can take advantage of Kaplans University Placement Service (UPS) which uses our extensive academic network to help you gain entry to graduate and undergraduate university courses across the country
